What is

Startup Aid

A 5-week program aimed at students to help structure their startup idea by connecting them with experts in the field and will leave the idea ready for incubation pitches. In the end you can pitch to CIE and receive funding.

What is a good idea?
There are no bad ideas or prerequisites.

     
     
     

Commut

Founded by six IIIT-H graduates, Commut is a tech-driven bus shuttle service that has been operational in Hyderabad since 2015 providing economical, reliable and hassle free transport to work. It was recently acquired by a Dubai based company, Careem that will be looking after their shuttle service operations in about 100 cities around the world.

DreamVu

DreamVu has developed the world's first omni-stereo camera hardware and software platform for unifying human and machine vision. Our technology has a billion fold advantage in capturing and processing visual information in comparison to the current solutions available in the market. With a wide range of applications DreamVu is set to revolutionize the way we capture reality.

WichPlease

Purpose behind starting WichPlease was hunger. Young foodies of IIIT-Hyderabad, Rahul sachdev, Pravin Gadodiya, Siddharth Jain and Saurabh Jain founded which please to overcome the problem of finding tasty, affordable and hygienic food options on a daily basis. Today WichPlease caters to as many as 200 plus customers in a day and many leave a positive review on Zomato thanking for the service and remembering for the food. It’s a standing proof that reflects commitment and careful execution of the once students of IIIT Hyderabad to today’s proud entrepreneurs.

Why

Startup Aid

The program has no prerequisites and is aimed at students. Just carry an idea and walk into the room. Incubation has never been easier. There is also an all-paid-for Singapore trip for selected people who finish the 5-week program. With teach you a lot: connect with faculty, alumni startups, experts from the field and learn about the startup market and culture.
